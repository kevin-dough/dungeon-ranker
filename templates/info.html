what is dungeon ranker?

dungeon ranker is a school project made by Kevin Do and Kian Kishimoto for their AP Computer Science Principles Digital Portfolio submission.

how is the score calculated?

Catacombs level and secrets both are out of 150 points. To get 150 points in each section you need catacombs 38 and 12,000 secrets.
Every catacombs level over is 1 bonus point and every 5,000 secrets over is a bonus point.
